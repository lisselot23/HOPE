Our Purpose

Depression is a serious issue that many teenage girls face. It makes them feel like they have no way out. It makes them feel inferior, worthless. It makes them feel like there is no hope. Our goal is to open up their eyes. We want to show them that they are not alone. That everything will be okay. That there is hope. However this issue not only occurs to teenage girls in America, it happens to teenage girls all around the world. For this reason we want to reach ot to others parts in the world. We want this to be an international help page. Girls all over the world need to know that they have someone who awaits them with open arms.

Where?

We've started with the USA, South Korea, and Latin America, however, we hope to increase our helplines and become worldwide.
